Founder

Aswin Subanthore, Ph.D.

Aswin Subanthore is the Founder of Rastera, a locational and behavioral intelligence platform focused on advanced geospatial analytics, AI-driven decision intelligence, and spatial insights for businesses, investors, and infrastructure planners.

With a strong background in GIS, geospatial analytics, and applied spatial intelligence, Aswin has worked across infrastructure, environmental, and analytics domains, helping organizations translate location data into strategic business decisions. Rastera reflects his vision of making powerful geospatial intelligence more accessible, actionable, and business-ready through AI and modern analytics.
