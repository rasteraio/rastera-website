Founder

Aswin Subanthore, Ph.D.

Aswin Subanthore, Ph.D. is the Founder and CEO of Rastera. He brings deep applied expertise in GIS, spatial AI, and geospatial analytics, with hands-on experience across infrastructure, environmental, and private sector analytics projects — translating complex location data into strategic decisions.

Rastera was built from a conviction that geospatial intelligence should be as accessible to a VP of Strategy as it is to a GIS specialist. Aswin's work spans the full stack of spatial analysis — from data engineering and environmental modeling to executive-facing dashboards and investment due diligence — giving Rastera a rare combination of technical depth and business focus.
