About Rastera

Locational & behavioral intelligence for a data-driven world

Rastera is a locational and behavioral intelligence platform delivering advanced geospatial analytics, spatial AI insights, and decision intelligence for businesses, investors, infrastructure planners, and organizations navigating complex geographic and market environments.

Founded in Virginia, USA, Rastera combines deep geospatial expertise with modern AI-driven analytics to transform location data into actionable business intelligence. The platform integrates environmental data, infrastructure context, consumer behavior insights, and predictive modeling to support site selection, expansion planning, risk analysis, and strategic investment decisions.

Mission

Beyond maps — toward measurable outcomes

Rastera's mission is to make powerful geospatial intelligence accessible, practical, and decision-ready — helping organizations move beyond maps toward measurable strategic outcomes.

Market Focus

Bridging GIS and business intelligence

Rastera focuses on bridging the gap between traditional GIS workflows and business decision-making by delivering scalable, modern geospatial intelligence tools tailored for enterprise, infrastructure, investment, and emerging data-driven industries.

Proudly headquartered in Virginia, Rastera serves clients globally while maintaining strong roots in applied geospatial analytics, infrastructure intelligence, and spatial AI innovation.
