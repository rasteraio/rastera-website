About Rastera

Building the AI solutions modern businesses need

Rastera is a full stack AI solutions company. We build production-ready AI-powered products — from location intelligence platforms and GeoAI tools to early-stage MVPs and SEO-optimized websites. Founded in Virginia by a geospatial scientist and applied AI practitioner, Rastera bridges the gap between AI capability and real-world business execution.

Where most AI projects stall in proof-of-concept, we ship. Where others deliver decks and prototypes, we deliver deployed, production-quality code that businesses can use on day one.

Mission

AI that ships, not just impresses

Rastera exists to make AI-powered products accessible — not just to enterprises with large engineering teams, but to founders, operators, and businesses that need real solutions fast. We bring full stack expertise and move at startup speed, without sacrificing production quality.

Who We Serve

From early-stage founders to established operators

Whether you need an MVP launched in weeks, a location intelligence platform for enterprise decisions, or a full SEO website built in a single day — Rastera operates at every stage of the business lifecycle. We scope precisely, build lean, and hand over code that's ready to scale.

Headquartered in Virginia and working with clients across sectors, Rastera is founded on deep applied expertise in spatial AI, full stack development, and production AI engineering. Our work spans public and private sector organizations — from infrastructure and real estate to early-stage startups finding their first product-market fit.
