About Rastera

The GeoAI platform for location decisions that move capital

Rastera is a GeoAI platform that turns location data into investment-grade intelligence for businesses, investors, and infrastructure planners. Where traditional GIS requires specialist teams and weeks of analysis, Rastera delivers structured, decision-ready outputs in hours.

Founded in Virginia, Rastera integrates demographic, environmental, infrastructure, and competitive data into a unified platform that supports site selection, expansion planning, risk assessment, and strategic investment decisions — without the friction of conventional geospatial tooling.

Mission

Beyond maps — toward measurable outcomes

Rastera exists to close the gap between where location data lives and where decisions get made. Our platform makes geospatial intelligence accessible to operators, investors, and planners — without requiring a GIS team.

Market Focus

Where GIS ends, Rastera begins

Traditional GIS tools were built for cartographers and specialists. Rastera is built for the people who deploy capital — executives, investment teams, and operators who need location context as part of their decision process, not a separate discipline.

Headquartered in Virginia and serving clients across sectors, Rastera is built on deep applied expertise in geospatial analytics, infrastructure intelligence, and spatial AI — grounded in real-world problem solving, not theoretical research.
